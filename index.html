Cities

The 3 main cities to visit

  • Tokyo

    The heart of Japan and the location for the 2021 Summer Olympics. This is where we will spend the majority of our time. Today, Tokyo offers a seemingly unlimited choice of shopping, entertainment, culture and dining to its visitors. The city's history can be appreciated in districts such as Asakusa and in many excellent museums, historic temples and gardens.

    Learn More

  • Kyoto

    The traditional side of Japan, Kyoto is what you imagine when you discuss this country. Its shrines and temples will leave anybody in awe. Countless temples, shrines and other historically priceless structures survive in the city today.

    Learn More

  • Osaka

    This is the second largest metropolitan area in Japan after Tokyo. It fuses together the traditional atmosphere commonly associated with Japan and the technologically advanced aspect Japan is known for.
